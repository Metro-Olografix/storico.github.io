the Italian Gathering: 1997 | 1998


We have decided to extend TiG'97 with some events that don't belong to a standard demo-party. TiG'97 will host some conferences about various interesting topics. Everyone can partecipate to those conference by listening or speaking. The conferences are opened to everyone. You can take the microphone and say whatever you want. There will be technical conferences and lighter conferences about non-specifical topics. All the conferences-related material will be released as party-productions.

Conference scheduling

  • Italian demoscene
    Coordinated by :
    Abstract : The Italian demoscene meets himself at TiG'97. Open discussion about the current status of the demoscene in Italy.

  • Amatorial Networks
    Coordinated by : Neur0
    Abstract : The current status of amatorial networks in Italy (FidoNet, PeaceLink, etc).

  • Introduction to the TCP/IP
    Coordinated by : Xenon (Nemesis Revived, TiG'97 Organizing)
    Abstract : Introduction to the TCP/IP networking. Protocols stack, inner workings, addresses, ARP, gateways.

  • IRC protection against IRC warriors
    Coordinated by : Spirit (Chaotic Visions/The Bermuda Triangle)
    Abstract : How to defend himself by the attacks of other people while chatting on the IRC.

If you have some knowledge about a specific topic and you want to be the coordinator of some conferences, you can send a mail to ig97@metro.it with the topics you want to cover and a brief description of you discussion.
