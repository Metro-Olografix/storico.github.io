How to reach TiG'97

Going on foot

Reaching the TiG'97 party-place is very simple because it is only 700m far away from the central railway station. When you exit from the station, you will see, in front of you, the city center. You will notice a lot of flags representing the italian railway company logo. A lot of taxis will be in front of you! You must go left and take the first turning on the left. Go straigth along that road (it is a sort of tunnel. You will walk under the first floor of the station). At the end of this road you will be just in front of "Via Aremogna". Your path is summarized by the green arrow on the map.
When you are on "Via Aremogna" you must go straight along that road. You will notice a restaurant on the left (it is called "Trattoria Fernando"), just before the crossing. Once you have arrived at the crossing, take the road that is in front of "Via Aremogna". It is "Via Passolanciano". You must go straight along that road until you reach another crossing. At this point you should notice a newspapers stand and a fuel station on the right. Turn right and you will be on "Via Rigopiano". This is the road where the party place is located. You must walk until you will see the party place on the right. The green arrow shows your path.

Coming by bus

If you carry a lot of stuff (computers and so on), you might also come to the party place by bus. Once you have arrived to the station, you can notice the bus stop in the big area just in front of the station exit. You must take the bus number 9. It will leave you just in front of the party place. Ask to the bus-driver when to get off.
